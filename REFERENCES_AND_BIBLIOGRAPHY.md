## NOTES ON BIBLIOGRAPHY

### Citation Style Used:
- **Harvard Reference Style** for academic books and papers
- **APA Format** for online resources and web standards
- **URLs** provided for online resources and official documentation

### How to Access References:
- **Academic Books**: Available through university library or online retailers
- **Online Documentation**: Direct URLs provided for web standards and framework docs
- **Research Papers**: Access through IEEE Xplore, ACM Digital Library, or ResearchGate
- **Official Specs**: W3C, OWASP, NIST resources are freely available online

### Recommended Citation Format for Report:
When citing in your thesis, use the following format:

**For Books:**
> Author, A. A., & Author, B. B. (Year). *Title of book*. Publisher. ISBN: xxx-xxxx

**For Online Resources:**
> Organization. (Year). *Title of resource*. Retrieved from URL

**For Journal Articles:**
> Author, A. A., Author, B. B., & Author, C. C. (Year). Title of article. *Journal Name*, Volume(Issue), Page numbers.
